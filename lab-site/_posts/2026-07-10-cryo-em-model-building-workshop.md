We hosted a hands-on workshop on building atomic models into intermediate-resolution
cryo-EM maps, led by Grzegorz Chojnowski. The session covered practical model building
in Coot and automated tracing with Doppio at around 5 Å resolution.

Thanks to everyone who came along, and to Grzegorz for making the trip to Newcastle.
