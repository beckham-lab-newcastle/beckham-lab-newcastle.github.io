Aaron Scott joined the group as a PhD student in September 2025. He comes to the
lab from Newcastle, where he completed his Integrated Masters investigating
Gram-positive protein secretion systems and related machineries.

His PhD explores novel outer membrane proteins in mycobacteria, combining cryo-EM
and biophysical techniques with bioinformatics, functional assays and -omics
screens. Welcome, Aaron!
