Harry recently attended the 7th Membrane Protein Workshop, organised by the
Biochemical Society and hosted at Aston University. The programme covered current
and emerging methods for studying membrane proteins biochemically, biophysically
and structurally — a good fit for his work on mycobacterial outer membrane
proteins.

Harry was awarded a **BBSRC Bursary** to attend. Our thanks to the Biochemical
Society for organising the workshop, and to the event sponsors, Cytiva and
Clinisciences.
