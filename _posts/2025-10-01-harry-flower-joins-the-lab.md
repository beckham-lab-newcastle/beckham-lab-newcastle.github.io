Harry Flower joined the group as a PhD student in October 2025, arriving in
Newcastle from the University of St Andrews, where he completed both his BSc and
MSc.

His project focuses on characterising mycobacterial outer membrane proteins —
combining microbiological, biochemical and biophysical approaches to work towards
their structures. Welcome, Harry!
