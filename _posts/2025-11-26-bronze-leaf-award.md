Following a recent audit, the Newcastle University Structural Biology Facility has
been awarded a **Bronze LEAF Award** in recognition of our commitment to
sustainable laboratory practices.

The LEAF programme (Laboratory Efficiency Assessment Framework) is a
sustainability initiative designed for research, teaching, and medical
laboratories. It provides a framework of practical actions that lab users can
implement to reduce energy consumption, minimise plastic and water usage, and
make more efficient use of other resources.

By participating in LEAF, we are reducing our environmental impact, lowering our
carbon emissions, and fostering an environment that supports high-quality
research while promoting responsible resource use.
