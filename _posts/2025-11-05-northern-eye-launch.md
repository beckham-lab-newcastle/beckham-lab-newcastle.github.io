An exciting day here in Newcastle celebrating the opening of the Northern Eye — a new 100 kV cryo-EM microscope and a great resource for structural biologists across the North East.

I also had the pleasure of speaking in the afternoon session, on the intrinsic flexibility of the mycobacterial Type VII secretion central pore and its role in substrate translocation.

For more about the facility, check out the [NUSBF website](https://nusbf.ncl.ac.uk/).
