Aaron attended the Wellcome/MRC-funded Cryo-EM in Structural Biology 2026 course,
hosted by the Astbury Biostructure Laboratory at the University of Leeds (2–6
March 2026). The hybrid course covered the theory behind cryo-EM and demonstrated
workflows for grid preparation, imaging and data processing, with hands-on
experience throughout.

Our thanks to the Astbury Biostructure Laboratory for arranging the course, and to
Wellcome and the MRC for funding the programme.
